About Me

Academic by day, action figure artisan by night. I master the meta of the classroom while crafting the heroes of tomorrow

Greetings! I'm Eljie M. Gorospe, a 19-year-old on a permanent quest to maximize my XP in both the academic and creative realms. I specialize in being a dual-wielder:

I'm prioritizing the 'Studying' buff to clear the highest-level academic dungeons, currently focused on BSIT Software Engineering. My goal is to achieve a 'World First' grade, using the logic and precision learned in my coursework to refine my craft.

When the textbooks are on cooldown, I switch to my creative specialization: Action Figure Making. You'll find me: Sculpting the Masters: Using 3D modeling software (CAD) to engineer articulation points and create detailed digital masters. Prototyping & Testing: Applying manufacturing knowledge to select the right materials and refine the mechanical functionality of my figures. Detailing the Lore: Every figure I make is a small-scale character brought to life, requiring meticulous attention to detail that’s just as important as clean code.
